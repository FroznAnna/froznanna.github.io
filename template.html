Watermelon Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 6 watermelons, quartered
  • 1 lasagna
  • 4 cm ginger
  • 2 mushrooms, diced
  • 250g sugarsnap peas
  • 5 gallons motor oil

Method

  1. Deep fry diced mushrooms
  2. Heat 1 tbsp motor oil in a saucepan, add sugarsnap peas and stir fry until black
  3. Eat the ginger to cleanse your palette
  4. Oil a baking dish and place the lasagna in the middle, surrounded by 3 of the watermelons.
  5. Bake the remaning watermelons until the flesh is soft

Notes

  • This is a generational recipe passed from my grandfather to my grandmother
  • If watermelon is not in season, then wait until it is
